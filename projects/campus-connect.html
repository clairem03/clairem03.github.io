The problem

Hundreds of clubs, no good way to find yours

Most campuses have hundreds of organizations, but discovery is dire — a wall of names in a directory tells a new student nothing about where they'd fit. The result is that plenty of great communities stay invisible to the people who'd love them.

Campus Connect makes discovery feel personal: browse organizations, and get recommendations that surface the ones worth a closer look. I built the mobile frontend and wired it to live data so the experience felt real, not static.
