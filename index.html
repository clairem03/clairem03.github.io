03 / About

About Me

My path into tech wasn't a straight line. I'm a software engineering senior at Iowa State with a minor in hospitality management — a pairing that shaped how I work. Engineering taught me to build things that work; hospitality taught me to care about how people actually feel using them.

Since then I've gotten to try a bit of everything: product owner and data co-op at Delta Air Lines, co-founder of an early-stage startup, and a peer mentor for 40+ first-year students. What I enjoy most is the problem-solving itself — figuring out what to build, how it should work, and then making it real.

Outside of class, you'll usually find me at the climbing gym, and when I'm not, I'm at the piano or painting. I'm graduating in December 2026 and looking for a team where I can keep learning and do work I'm proud of.
